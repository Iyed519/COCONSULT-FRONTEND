Standard ISO 9001

what is?

ISO 9001 is an international standard that defines the requirements for establishing a Quality Management System (QMS). iso page.

1. Process-Based Approach

ISO promotes a process approach to improve efficiency and operational management. This involves:

Identifying and understanding key organizational processes.
Continuously improving these processes for optimal results.
Connecting and integrating processes for effective management.


Consistently seeking better practices.
Adapting to internal and external changes. Using tools such as PDCA (Plan-Do-Check-Act) to ensure ongoing enhancement

3. Risk-Based Thinking

Many ISO standards, such as ISO 31000 (Risk Management), emphasize risk-based thinking, which includes:

Identifying potential risks and opportunities.
Implementing strategies to mitigate risks.
Enhancing decision-making through proactive risk management.
